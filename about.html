About Me

Hi, I’m Brendan Reeves, a Stanford alum with a Bachelor’s in Symbolic Systems and currently pursuing a Master’s in Computer Science, focusing on AI. I’m passionate about how technology and human behavior intersect, from building research models to helping companies build innovative AI policy. Outside of tech, you can catch me singing with Stanford Mendicants, exploring new cultures, or thinking of new ways to make language learning more accessible.
