Bina — Free and Effective Language Learning for All!

Bina helps you master a new language by prioritizing practical, everyday speech rather than obscure vocabulary and grammar rules. We focus on the most common 500 words used in daily conversation, guided by the Zipfian distribution of language frequency.

While traditional methods overemphasize written language and grammar charts, Bina encourages learners to start by speaking first, leveraging the natural ability of our brains for statistical learning and pattern recognition. This is referred to in the Linguistics literature as leveraging “large-to-small grain processing” (Arnon 2021) with promising evidence.

With a flexible, user-driven system that updates based on what’s most relevant to the learner, Bina makes language acquisition faster, more intuitive, and more efficient, all while de-emphasizing early writing to focus on fluent communication.

Code is not currently open source. Please reach out if this project sounds exciting to you!
